Here is my Jammin' program in action! Below is a picture of the setup display the switch connection. I struggled forever as the board was not playing anything let alone a full song. Eventually I decided to reset it and we were immidiatley in buisness. Thus my advice to myself is that if it appears it should be working it probably is — reset your board! After getting the test tone to play I had one of my roomates who is a musical whiz help me program the song. It is a version of Fire on The Mountain (without drums and with rough timing), but we had fun doing it!
